# Boundary

OMNIABASE follows the ecosystem boundary:

    measurement != inference != decision

Strictly speaking, OMNIABASE often sits even earlier:

    observation != measurement != inference != decision

---

## Meaning

Observation means:

    extract representation-dependent or representation-stable structure.

Measurement means:

    quantify or evaluate structural behavior under declared conditions.

Inference means:

    interpret the signal.

Decision means:

    act on the interpretation.

OMNIABASE primarily supports observation and representation-aware preparation.

---

## Allowed claims

OMNIABASE may claim:

- this object was represented in multiple bases;
- this feature appeared in a representation;
- this feature changed across representations;
- this feature remained stable across declared representations;
- this representation shift exposed or removed a property.

---

## Disallowed claims

OMNIABASE should not claim by itself:

- this is semantically true;
- this is metaphysically true;
- this is a final structural proof;
- this replaces OMNIA measurement;
- this replaces OMNIA-VALIDATION;
- this downstream decision is correct.

---

## Rule

The output must preserve the distinction:

    representation observation
    structural measurement
    interpretation
    external decision
